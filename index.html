About

Arizona Men's Derby is the premier men’s derby team in Arizona. We are currently recruiting skaters of all skill levels to join our team. The only requirements are to have a desire to be a part of the sport, be 18+, have a great attitude, and be able to skate or learn how to skate on QUAD SKATES! If you are interested in joining us or know someone who is you are welcome to come out to a practice and see what it’s all about!
